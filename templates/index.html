Predict Air Quality

All values typically range from 0 to 500.

SO2 (Sulfur Dioxide)

A colorless and pungent gas, poses significant threats to human health and the environment, primarily through respiratory problems and the formation of acid rain.

NO2 (Nitrogen Dioxide)

A reddish-brown gas, is a harmful air pollutant that contributes to the formation of smog, respiratory issues, and has adverse effects on the environment and climate.

RSPM (Respirable Suspended Particulate Matter)

A hazardous airborne pollutant consisting of tiny solid particles that can penetrate deep into the lungs, leading to severe respiratory and cardiovascular health problems.

SPM (Suspended Particulate Matter)

A mixture of solid and liquid particles suspended in the air, which can have harmful effects on human health and the environment.
